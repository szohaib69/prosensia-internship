Twitter

Twitter is a microblogging platform that allows users to send and read short posts called "tweets." It's used for real-time updates, news, and social interactions

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social networking service that allows users to share posts and stories. It's known for its visual focus and strong influencer culture.

YouTube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform where users can upload, view, and share videos. Its a popular platform for entertainment, education, and marketing.
